Welcome to the SkyCity Webpage! Here you can find a list of all the items we sell, as well as well we can supply you. This is in three yield tiers: Low, Medium, High. This output is based relative to how hard it is to get these items without a farm, as well as how well we can supply a user. For example, our Cobblestone farm can produce 4 figures worth of cobblestone per hour, much more than a single person can obtain in that time, and plenty to supply a mega-build. Thus it is a "High-Yield" farm. On the other end, our blackstone farm is a low-yield farm, only producing slightly more blackstone per hour as someone manually mining. As another example, a trident farm that produces 2 tridents per hour is a High-Yield farm, since that is faster than sitting in an ocean with Looting III, and 2 tridents will last the average player quite a long time. Also, if you're searching for anything in particular, CTRL + F will allow you to search :)
